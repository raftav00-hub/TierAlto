Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you fill out a form on this website, including your name, email address, company name, phone number, and any details you include in form fields. We do not collect any information passively through tracking technologies beyond standard web analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry and, if an engagement begins, to conduct the work we are engaged to do. We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We retain inquiry information for a reasonable period to allow us to respond and follow up appropriately. If no engagement begins, inquiry data is typically deleted within 12 months. If you would like your information removed sooner, contact us at hello@tieralto.com.
