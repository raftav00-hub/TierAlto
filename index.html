The Provisioning Reality

The Problem Nobody Talks About

Zero Touch Provisioning works great — until you have devices from multiple vendors. Cisco ZTP won't touch your Yealink phones. Poly's provisioning platform doesn't know your Grandstream devices exist. The moment you have a mixed-vendor environment — or you're migrating from one vendor to another — the automation breaks down and someone has to configure devices manually.

That someone is us. Vendor-neutral, platform-agnostic, and experienced with the exact mixed-vendor complexity that ZTP can't solve.
