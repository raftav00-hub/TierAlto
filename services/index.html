Expert Technology Consulting - From Strategy to Delivery

TierAlto offers five core service lines, each designed to solve a specific problem that channel partners, distributors, and mid-market companies face with communications technology. Every engagement comes with the same thing: one team, one point of accountability, one outcome.

The Problem with How Provisioning Works Today

Most provisioning processes were designed around a single vendor’s platform. When that worked, nobody questioned it. But the channel has never been single-vendor, and the platforms that channel partners actually use - RingCentral, Cisco Webex, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Broadworks - each have their own provisioning models. When you add a second device brand, or migrate from one platform to another, the whole system breaks.

The people who get called in to fix it are usually the vendor’s tier 2 support team - who know their product, but not your environment. Or a generalist IT consultant who knows a bit about everything and not enough about this. Or no one, and the problem just festers.

TierAlto was built for exactly this gap. Deep, specialized expertise in device provisioning, SIP, VoIP, and unified communications - combined with vendor neutrality and a commitment to actually delivering the outcome.

  • We work across all major device brands: Poly, Yealink, Grandstream, Cisco, Avaya, AudioCodes, Fanvil
  • We work across all major platforms: RingCentral, Microsoft Teams, Zoom Phone, Webex, Broadworks, 3CX
  • We do not sell hardware. We do not get commissions. Our only interest is solving your problem.
  • Every engagement is scoped, priced, and delivered by the same person who took the first call
  • We document everything so your team can operate independently after we are done
Service 1

Pre-Shipment Device Configuration

Device personalization before it ever leaves the warehouse.

Before a phone ships, it should already know who it is. Its extension. Its platform. Its firmware version. Its display name. Pre-shipment configuration means the device is plug-and-play from the moment it arrives - no on-site technician required, no configuration portal login, no manual setup per device.

TierAlto handles this at any scale. Whether you are staging ten devices for a small office or ten thousand for a national rollout, we apply the configuration, validate the result, and ship devices that are ready to work.

What is included

  • Device-by-device configuration per your MAC/extension mapping
  • Firmware load and validation
  • Platform-specific provisioning (SIP, RingCentral, Teams, Webex, etc.)
  • Custom display names, time zones, and feature key layout
  • Pre-ship validation testing
  • Shipping coordination if needed

Priced per device, with volume discounts for larger batches.

Service 2

Provisioning Workflow Design

Built to own. Not locked into someone else’s platform.

Most channel partners and distributors rely on a vendor’s managed provisioning service because they have not had the option to build their own. That means their provisioning operation is constrained by what the vendor supports, priced at whatever the vendor charges, and exposed to whatever changes the vendor decides to make.

TierAlto designs provisioning workflows you own. The templates, the logic, the sequencing - built for your operation and portable across vendors and platforms.

What is included

  • Current-state workflow assessment
  • Architecture design for a vendor-neutral provisioning model
  • Template and configuration file design
  • Sequencing logic for mixed-vendor environments
  • Documentation for your operations team
  • Handoff and training session

Fixed-fee project. Scope and price agreed before work begins.

Service 3

UC Platform Advisory

Vendor-neutral guidance from engineers who’ve seen it all.

Choosing a UC platform is not just a licensing decision. It is a device decision, a network decision, a support decision, and an integration decision all rolled into one. Most of the guidance available comes from people who have a stake in which platform you choose.

TierAlto has no platform to sell and no vendor relationship to protect. Our team has worked inside the ecosystem long enough to know where the bodies are buried - from a team that has worked inside these systems at enterprise scale for decades - which platforms handle mixed-device environments well, which ones have provisioning limitations that only surface after deployment, and which ones are genuinely the right fit for different types of organizations.

What is included

  • Current environment assessment
  • Platform evaluation against your specific requirements
  • Device compatibility analysis
  • Migration path planning
  • Risk identification and mitigation strategy
  • Written recommendation with supporting rationale

Advisory retainer or fixed-fee project, depending on scope.
